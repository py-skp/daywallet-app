DayWallet Support

Get help with DayWallet, find answers to common questions, and contact support if you need anything.

How can we help?

DayWallet is designed to work simply: no account, no bank connection, and no cloud syncing. If something is unclear, the answers below should help.

Does DayWallet connect to my bank?

No. DayWallet works without bank connections. You enter your balance, bills, and income yourself, and the app calculates your safe-to-spend number locally on your device.

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device. DayWallet does not require an account and does not sync your data to the cloud.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Open DayWallet, go to Settings, and tap Upgrade. Premium unlocks unlimited bills, unlimited income sources, full history, CSV export, and removes ads.

What happens if I delete the app?

Because your data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app may remove your stored data. Export anything important before uninstalling.

How is Safe to Spend calculated?

DayWallet estimates what you can safely spend based on your current balance, bills due before payday, your chosen safety buffer, and the number of days until your next income.

Can I track more than one income source?

Yes. You can track salary, freelance work, side income, rent, or other expected income. Free and Premium plans may have different limits depending on your version.

Troubleshooting

  • If something looks wrong, close and reopen the app first.
  • Check that your balance, bills, income dates, and safety buffer are up to date.
  • If Premium is not showing correctly, reopen the Settings screen and try again.
  • Before deleting the app, export your data if you may need it later.
